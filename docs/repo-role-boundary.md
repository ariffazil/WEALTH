# WEALTH — Repo Role Boundary

> Role: Economic Topology Engine
> Anti-Sink Responsibility: Detect extractive capital flows, ownership concentration

---

## Invariant Role

WEALTH is the **capital intelligence and economic topology engine** of the federation. It:
- Analyzes capital flows, risk, allocation, and stewardship
- Detects extractive economic topologies (ownership concentration, rent capture)
- Evaluates whether wealth creation distributes or concentrates agency
- Provides financial intelligence within constitutional bounds

## Must Never Become

- An unchecked allocator of irreversible capital moves (requires sovereign)
- A governance kernel (that is arifOS)
- A control plane (that is AAA)

## Core Question

> Does this wealth flow create agency or dependence?

## Anti-Sink Diagnostics

WEALTH should eventually implement:
- Ownership concentration scoring (Gini coefficient for capital access)
- Capital chokepoint detection
- Inclusive access scoring per economic domain
- Dependency risk scoring (does a financial relationship create or reduce agency?)
- Role-creation metric (does capital enable new differentiated roles?)
